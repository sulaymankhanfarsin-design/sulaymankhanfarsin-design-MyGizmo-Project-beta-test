About Farsin OS

We believe in building simple, powerful tools for complex problems.

Farsin OS started as a simple idea: "What if all the small web tools we use every day were in one place, beautifully designed, and fast?"

This project, built by Farsin Khan, is dedicated to creating that premium, all-in-one workspace. From file converters to image editors, Farsin OS is designed to boost your productivity and streamline your workflow.

We are just getting started. Our plan is to build the most comprehensive and user-friendly toolkit on the web, available to you with a simple subscription.
